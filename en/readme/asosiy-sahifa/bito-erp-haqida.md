# About Bito ERP



### What is Bito ERP?

Bito ERP is a comprehensive management system designed for small and medium-sized retail and wholesale businesses, enabling the automation of trade, warehouse, procurement, finance, production, CRM, and distribution processes on a single platform.

The system simplifies daily business operations, reduces human errors, and provides management with deep analytical reports in real-time.





### Who is it for?

Bito ERP is highly effective for the following types of businesses:

* Retail stores
* Wholesale companies
* Multi-branch businesses
* Warehouse centers
* Manufacturing enterprises
* Distribution networks

If your business involves product movement, sales control, and financial accounting — Bito ERP is a perfect fit.





### How does Bito ERP work?

Bito ERP is designed to manage all processes in small and medium-sized businesses—from product movement to financial results—in a single system.

**Warehouse — product movement control**

Every product's receipt, release, and balance is visible in real-time. Inter-warehouse transfers, inventory, and cost calculations are automatically handled by the system.

As a result:

* Balance errors decrease
* Losses are identified
* Product movement becomes transparent

**Sales — revenue management**

Order creation, contract management, returns, and payment control work as a unified process. Discounts and additional expenses are automatically accounted for.

As a result:

* Sales accelerate
* Debt is under control
* Human errors decrease



**Procurement — systematizing purchases**

Working with suppliers, creating purchase orders, and controlling purchase prices form an accurate product cost.

As a result:

* Purchases are planned
* Price control strengthens
* Unnecessary expenses decrease



**Finance — seeing real profit**

Cash and bank operations, income and expenses, debt, and profit-loss reports are managed in a single system. Profitability can be viewed by branches.

As a result:

* Management sees real financial results
* Decision-making accelerates



**Production — cost control**

Raw material consumption, production orders, and finished product acceptance processes are systematically managed. Cost is automatically calculated.

As a result:

* Complete control over cost
* Losses decrease



**CRM — strategic customer work**

Each customer's purchase history, debt, and activity are visible. Customer segmentation is possible.

As a result:

* Opportunity to increase sales emerges
* Customer work becomes systematic



**Distribution — territorial control**

Sales agents, regional orders, and delivery processes are monitored.

As a result:

* Distribution becomes transparent
* Sales representative efficiency is measured





### Reports and analytics

Bito ERP not only manages operational processes but also provides management-level analytical insights based on collected data. The system shows the true state of the business through indicators updated in real-time.

The analytics module helps management find answers not only to "what happened?" but also to "why did it happen?" and "what should be done next?"

**Sales analytics**

Sales activity is analyzed across several dimensions:

* Daily, monthly, and annual sales dynamics
* Sales volume by products
* Best-selling and least-selling products
* Sales efficiency by managers
* Revenue by branches

Through this data:

* Growth or decline trends are identified
* Which product is the main revenue source is visible
* Sales plans are formed with solid foundation



**Profitability and profit analysis**

The system links revenue and expenses to show real profit.

* Product profitability
* Profit by branches
* Profit by customer segments
* Overall profit and loss (P&L)

Through this analysis:

* Which products are profitable or unprofitable is determined
* Branch efficiency is compared
* Pricing policy can be optimized



**Warehouse analytics**

Warehouse movements are deeply analyzed:

* Inventory turnover rate
* Frozen stock level
* Products with no significant movement
* Products below minimum stock

As a result:

* Turnover rate increases
* Capital "freezing" decreases
* Excess inventory decreases



**Customer analytics (CRM)**

Customers are segmented based on behavior:

* Active customers
* Passive customers
* Debtor customers
* Highest purchasing segments

Through this analysis:

* Marketing strategy is formed
* Individual offers can be made
* Customer retention rate increases



**Expense and cash flow analysis**

Financial movements are shown by structure:

* Expense categories
* Monthly expense trends
* Cash and bank flow
* Accounts receivable and payable

This data enables:

* Expense optimization
* Cash flow management
* Financial stability assurance
