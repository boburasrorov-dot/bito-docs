# Purchases



### Creating Purchases

When the **Create** button is pressed in the Supply → Purchases section, a new purchase creation window opens.\
Through this section, receiving products from suppliers, warehouse receipts, and managing payment processes are carried out. Through the Purchases section, all real operations — product receipts, additional expenses, and financial calculations are managed in a unified system.

The purchase creation process consists of several stages:

#### **Basic Information**

At this stage, general and basic information about the purchase is entered.

The following information is entered:

• **Purchase Order** — an existing purchase order is selected.\
• **P Number** — the purchase document number is entered.\
• **Contract** — the contract concluded with the supplier is selected.\
• **Status** — the purchase status is set.\
• **Date** — the purchase creation date is entered.\
• **Receipt Date** — the date when the product will be received into the warehouse is set.\
• **Responsible Person** — the employee responsible for the purchase is assigned.\
• **Supplier** — the product supplier is selected.\
• **Currency** — the currency is selected.\
• **Direct Receipt** — the function to immediately receive products into the warehouse is enabled or disabled.\
• **Warehouse** — the warehouse where the product will be received is selected (if direct receipt is enabled).\
• **Upload File** — documents related to the purchase are attached.\
• **Note** — additional information or comments are written.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (74).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### **Products**

At this stage, the products being purchased are entered and their quantities are formed.

The following information is entered:

• **Name** — the product is selected.\
• **Barcode** — the product code is displayed.\
• **Unit of Measure** — the product's unit of measure is displayed.\
• **Available Quantity** — the available balance in the warehouse is displayed.\
• **Quantity** — the quantity being purchased is entered.\
• **Box Quantity** — the quantity by packaging is entered.\
• **Total Quantity** — the total quantity is calculated.

Additional features:

• **Price Type** — selecting the product price (for example: last price and others).\
• **Auto-fill** — quick product fill function.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (75).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

As a result, all products and their quantities are formed.

#### **Additional Expenses**

At this stage, additional expenses related to the purchase are entered and their impact on the product cost is calculated.

The following information is entered:

• **Name** — expense name (transportation, logistics, and others).\
• **Currency** — the expense currency is selected.\
• **Affects Price** — whether the expense is added to the product cost is set.\
• **Distribution Method** — how the expense is distributed among products is selected.\
• **Recommended Price** — the value recommended by the system.\
• **Maximum Price** — the maximum allowed value.\
• **Price** — the actual expense amount is entered.

At the bottom, automatically displayed are:

• Total Quantity\
• Total Price\
• Total Weight and Volume

As a result, the final cost of products is formed.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (77).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### **Payment**

At this stage, financial settlement with the supplier is carried out.

The system automatically displays:

• **Balance** — available funds\
• **Discount** — discount given\
• **Amount** — total purchase amount\
• **Paid** — amount paid\
• **Debt** — remaining debt\
• **Next Balance** — balance after the operation

Payment details:

• **Cash Register** — the cash register where the payment will be made is selected.

In the payment methods list:

• **Payment Method** — cash, card, and others are selected.\
• **Amount** — the payment amount is entered.\
• **Currency** — the currency is selected.\
• **Exchange Value** — the conversion value is entered (if necessary).

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (76).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Additional feature:

• **Debit** — partially making the payment and leaving the remaining part as debt.

After all stages are completed and saved, the purchase is finalized.\
As a result, products are received into the warehouse, additional expenses are accounted for, and financial settlement with the supplier is fully formed.
