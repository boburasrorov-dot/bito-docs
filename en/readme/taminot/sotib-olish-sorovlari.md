# Purchase Requests

<details open>

<summary>Creating purchase requests</summary>

In the Supply → Purchase Requests section, when the **Create** button is pressed, a new purchase request window opens.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (59).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (68).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Through this section, requests for products and raw materials for enterprise needs are formed.

When creating a purchase request, the following information is entered:

• **PR number** — the request identification number is entered\
• **Status** — the request status is selected (new and others).\
• **Date** — the date the request was created is specified.\
• **Due date** — the deadline by which the request must be fulfilled is entered.\
• **Responsible person** — the employee responsible for the request is designated.\
• **Reason** — the reason for the purchase is selected.\
• **File upload** — documents related to the request are attached.\
• **Comment** — additional information or notes are written.

Through the products and raw materials section, the following information is entered:

• **Name** — the product or raw material is selected.\
• **Unit of measure** — the product unit of measure is indicated.\
• **Quantity** — the required quantity is entered.\
• **Box quantity** — the quantity per package is entered.\
• **Total quantity** — the total requirement is calculated.\
• **Reason** — the reason for this specific product is indicated.\
• **Note** — additional comments related to the product are written.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (69).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After all information is entered, it is saved and the purchase request is added to the system.\
As a result, enterprise needs are systematically formed and a basis for subsequent procurement processes is created.

</details>
