# Purchase Reasons



### Creating purchase reasons

In the Supply → Purchase Reasons section, clicking the **Create** button opens a new reason creation window.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (65).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (51).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This section allows you to predefine reasons for purchasing products. This makes it easier to analyze purchasing processes, view reports by reason categories, and facilitate decision-making.

When creating a purchase reason, the following information is entered:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (50).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

• **Name** — the purchase reason name is entered (for example: product out of stock, low inventory, etc.).\
• **Code** — a specific code for the reason is entered (optional).\
• **Sort order** — determines the display order in the reasons list.\
• **Comment** — additional information or explanation is written.\
• **Active** — indicates whether the reason is active or inactive.

After all information is entered, it is saved and the reason is added to the system.

As a result, purchases are conducted based on specific reasons, and this data enables the analysis, planning, and optimization of purchasing processes.
