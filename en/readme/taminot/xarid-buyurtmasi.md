# Purchase Order

<details open>

<summary>Creating a purchase order</summary>

In the Supply → Purchase Order section, when the **Create** button is pressed, a new purchase order window opens.

Through this section, official orders are formed for suppliers based on enterprise needs and procurement processes are planned. The purchase order allows for pre-ordering products, negotiating prices, and systematically managing the supply process. Additionally, through this section, future purchases are monitored and all orders are maintained in a unified system.

When creating a purchase order, the following information is entered:

• **PO number** — order number is entered \
• **Status** — order status is selected.\
• **Date** — the date the order was created is specified.\
• **Purchase date** — planned purchase date is entered.\
• **Responsible person** — responsible employee for the order is assigned.\
• **Supplier** — product supplier is selected.\
• **Currency** — currency is selected.\
• **File upload** — documents are attached.\
• **Comment** — additional information is entered.

Through the purchase requests section, existing requests are attached to the order.

Through the additional products and raw materials section:

• **Name** — product is selected.\
• **Unit of measure** — unit of measure is indicated.\
• **Quantity** — order quantity is entered.\
• **Box quantity** — package quantity is entered.\
• **Total quantity** — total volume is calculated.\
• **Price** — product price is entered.\
• **Total price** — total amount is calculated.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (70).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After all information is entered, it is saved and the purchase order is created.\
As a result, an official order to be sent to the supplier is formed and the procurement process is managed based on a clear plan.
