# Invoice

<details open>

<summary>Invoice</summary>

Through the **Invoice** section, financial operations related to customers are conducted. This section works similarly to the cash register, but operations are performed based on a **document (invoice)**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (413).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### Invoice List

In this section, all invoices are displayed in table format and include the following information:

* **Number** – invoice identifier
* **Date** – creation date
* **Organization** – the organization to which the invoice belongs
* **Income** – amount received by invoice
* **Expense** – expense by invoice
* **Paid** – actual amount paid
* **Status** – (Completed and others)
* **Payment Type** – operation type (sales, inventory and others)
* **Created By** – user who created the operation

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (41).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

In the invoice section, just like in the cash register:

* **Income** – record cash receipt by invoice
* **Expense** – formalize cash expense by invoice

meaning full control of cash flows is possible here as well.
