# Transactions



### Transactions

Apart from the cash register section, all transactions can also be viewed in a **separate section**. Through this section, cash movements across all cash registers in the system are displayed in a single table.

Accessed via **Finance → Transactions** section

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (412).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

In this section, operations performed across all cash registers are shown in a general table.

The table consists of the following information:

* **Number** – transaction identifier
* **Date** – operation date
* **Created time / Completed date**
* **Income / Expense** – cash movement amount
* **Payment method** – cash, terminal, and others
* **Status** – (Completed, Pending, and others)
* **Payment type** – operation content
* **Cashier** – user who performed the operation
* **Comment** – additional information
