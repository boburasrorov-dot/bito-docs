# Contracts

<details open>

<summary>Contracts</summary>

The Contracts section is used to create and manage financial agreements with third parties in the system. This section enables the formalization and control of loans, payment obligations, and other financial operations.

Users can view, filter, export all contracts, and create new contracts here.

#### Creating a Contract

A new contract is added through the **"Create"** button, where the following information is entered:

* **Code** — unique identifier for the contract (optional, but important for organization)
* **Name** — contract name or brief description
* **Number** — official contract number (for document tracking)
* **Issue Date** — the date the contract was concluded
* **Expiration Date** — contract validity period (if applicable)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (459).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### Contract Subject and Financial Section

* **3rd Party** — the external entity with whom the contract is being concluded (partner, debtor, or other party)
* **Currency** — the currency in which the contract is conducted
* **Amount** — contract sum (loan or obligation amount)

#### Additional Information

* **Comment** — additional information or notes regarding the contract
* **Status** — indicates whether the contract is active (in effect) or inactive (completed/canceled)
