# Cashback



### Cashback

The **CRM → Cashback** section displays information about cashbacks issued to customers and their usage.

Through this section, businesses can monitor cashback policies and analyze customer loyalty.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (83).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (84).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The top section shows overall cashback statistics:

• **Total cashback** — the current cashback amount available in customers' accounts\
• **Used cashback** — the amount of cashback used by customers\
• **Received cashback** — the total cashback amount issued to customers

This allows you to:

• see how much cashback has been issued\
• see how much has been used\
• see how much remains unused



**Cashback list**

The table below shows cashback by customer:

• **Customer** — customer name\
• **Phone number** — for contacting the customer\
• **Cashback** — cashback amount in the customer's account

This allows you to:

• see how much cashback each customer has\
• identify customers who have accumulated the most cashback
