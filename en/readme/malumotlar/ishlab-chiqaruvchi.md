# Manufacturer



### Creating a Manufacturer

The Manufacturer section serves to store and manage product manufacturers (suppliers or manufacturing entities) in the system. Through this section, users can maintain manufacturers associated with products in a unified database and later link them to products. This simplifies tracking and analyzing product origins.

Navigate to Data → Manufacturer section and add a new manufacturer via the **"Create"** button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (12).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

In this section, all manufacturers are displayed in table view.

#### Table columns:

* **Name** – manufacturer name
* **Type** – whether it is an individual or legal entity
* **Entity** – selected from existing individuals or legal entities
* **Name** – manufacturer name (entered automatically or manually)



<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (13).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This section operates in conjunction with other data (legal and individual entities). Therefore, when adding a manufacturer, existing entities are used to prevent data duplication. This maintains data integrity in the system and simplifies management.
