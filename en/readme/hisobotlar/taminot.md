<summary>Product Cost Price</summary>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (126).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This report displays the cost price of products. Through the report, you can view which warehouse the products were received into, when they were received, their initial cost price, quantity, and total value. This report helps analyze the receipt prices of products and their value in the warehouse.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (127).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**The report table contains the following columns:**

**№** — serial number in the table.

**Product** — product name.

**Unit of Measurement** — the unit in which the product is measured (for example: liter, kilogram).

**Organization** — the organization or branch to which the product belongs.

**Warehouse** — the name of the warehouse where the product is stored.

**Created Time** — the date and time when the product entry was entered into the system.

**Receipt Date** — the date when the product was received into the warehouse.

**Initial Cost Price** — the initial cost price of the product at the time of receipt.

**Quantity** — the quantity of product received into the warehouse.

**Cost Price** — the cost price of a single product.

**Total** — the total value based on the product quantity and cost price.



**Business Benefits**

Using this report, you can:

* monitor the receipt cost price of products
* view the total value of products in the warehouse
* analyze the receipt history of products
* control warehouse accounting and product cost price
