# HR



### Sales by Employee

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (245).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This report displays sales performance metrics by employees. Through the report, you can analyze how much sales each employee made, returned products, discounts, taxes, and final revenue. This report helps evaluate the sales process from an employee perspective.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (244).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**The report table contains the following columns:**

**№** — serial number in the table.

**Name** — name of the employee who made the sale.

**Sold** — number of sales made by the employee.

**Sold quantity** — total quantity of products sold.

**Returned quantity** — quantity of products returned by customers.

**Sales** — total sales amount of sold products.

**Returned** — total value of returned products.

**Discount** — amount of discounts applied during sales.

**Input tax** — amount of input tax calculated during sales.

**Added tax** — amount of value added tax calculated during sales.

**Revenue** — net revenue received from sales.

**Cost** — total cost of sold products.

**Average check** — average revenue amount per one sale.

**New customers count** — number of new customers attracted by the employee.

**Gross profit** — total profit after cost is accounted for.



**Business Benefits**

With this report, you can:

* analyze sales performance by employees
* compare sales volume and revenue across employees
* monitor returned products and discounts
* evaluate employee productivity in the sales process
* determine the effectiveness of attracting new customers
