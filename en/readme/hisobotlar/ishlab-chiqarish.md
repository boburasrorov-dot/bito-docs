# Production

<details>

<summary>Product Manufacturing</summary>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (131).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This report displays manufactured products for the selected time period, the raw materials consumed for them, and the production cost. The report allows analyzing which products were manufactured and in what quantities during the production process, and how many resources were used for production.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (132).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The report table contains the following columns related to the production process:

**№** — sequence number in the table.

**Product** — name of the manufactured product.

**Category** — category to which the product belongs.

**Unit of measurement** — unit in which the product is measured (e.g., liter, kilogram).

**Used quantity** — amount of raw materials consumed in the product manufacturing process.

**Used cost** — total cost of consumed raw materials.

**Manufactured quantity** — quantity of finished manufactured product.

**Manufacturing cost** — total cost of manufactured products.

**Unit product cost** — cost of manufacturing one unit of product.

**Additional expenses** — additional expenses added to the production process.

**Final price** — total final cost of product manufacturing.



**Business Benefits**

Using this report, you can:

* monitor raw materials consumed in the production process
* analyze product manufacturing costs
* see which products were manufactured and in what quantities
* assess production efficiency

</details>

<summary>Production by Responsible Person</summary>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (133).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This report displays the production process by responsible employees. The report allows analyzing how many products were manufactured by which employee, the raw materials consumed for production, and the production cost.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (134).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The report table contains the following columns related to the production process:

**№** — sequence number in the table.

**Responsible person** — name of the employee responsible for the production process.

**Used quantity** — amount of raw materials consumed in the product manufacturing process.

**Used cost** — total cost of consumed raw materials.

**Manufactured quantity** — quantity of product manufactured by the responsible employee.

**Manufacturing cost** — total cost of manufactured products.

**Additional expenses** — additional expenses added to the production process.



**Business Benefits**

Using this report, you can:

* analyze production efficiency by employees
* monitor resources consumed in the production process
* evaluate employee work performance
* control production costs

</details>

<summary>Recommended Raw Material Quantity</summary>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (128).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This report shows the recommended raw material quantity needed for product manufacturing. The report calculates the average consumption of products based on the selected **sales history** and helps determine how much raw material will be needed for upcoming days. Before using the report, you need to select the **sales history** through the filter.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (129).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**The report table contains the following columns:**

**№** — sequence number in the table.

**Category** — category to which the product belongs.

**Product** — name of the product used in manufacturing.

**Unit of measurement** — unit in which the product is measured (e.g., kilogram, liter).

**Warehouse quantity** — current raw material quantity available in the warehouse.

**Average usage** — average consumption of the product based on the selected sales history.

**Days sufficient** — shows for how many days the available warehouse raw materials will last for production.

**6-day recommendation** — recommended raw material quantity for the next 6 days.

**10-day recommendation** — recommended raw material quantity for the next 10 days.

**30-day recommendation** — recommended raw material quantity for the next 30 days.

**End of month recommendation** — recommended raw material quantity for production until the end of the month.



**Business Benefits**

Using this report, you can:

* plan the required raw material quantity for production in advance
* prevent raw material shortages
* efficiently manage product inventory in the warehouse
* identify necessary resources to maintain continuous production process



</details>

<summary>Product Manufacturing by Employees</summary>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (130).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This report displays manufactured products by employees. The report allows analyzing which products were manufactured by which employee and in what quantities, and the total production volume.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (243).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**The report table contains the following columns:**

**№** — sequence number in the table.

**Responsible person** — name of the employee responsible for the production process.

**Product name** (e.g., Apple juice) — displays the quantity of product manufactured by the employee.

**Total quantity** — total quantity of all products manufactured by the employee.



**Business Benefits**

Using this report, you can:

* analyze product quantity manufactured by employees
* evaluate employee production efficiency
* identify which products were manufactured most by whom
* control the production process by employees

</details>
