# Distribution

<details open>

<summary>Visit Report</summary>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (246).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This report displays general information about visits conducted by sales agents. Through the report, you can analyze agents' planned visits, completed visits, orders, sales results, and the success rate of visits.

**The report includes the following key indicators:**

**Visits (by visits)** — the overall percentage of visits conducted by agents.

**Successful visits** — the percentage of visits that resulted in an order or sale.

**Visits (via GPS)** — the percentage of visits confirmed via GPS.

**Photo reports** — the share of photo reports uploaded during visits.

**Total amount** — the sum of sales conducted within the selected date range.

**Completed sales** — the number of sales conducted during visits.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (247).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (248).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**The report table contains the following columns:**

**№** — serial number in the table.

**Employee** — the agent or sales employee who conducted the visits.

**Planned** — the number of visits planned for the agent.

**Visit** — the number of visits conducted by the agent.

**No visit** — the number of visits that were planned but not conducted.

**Photo report** — the number of photo reports uploaded during the visit.

**Sales** — the sales amount conducted during the visit.

**Quantity** — the total quantity of products sold.

**Unsuccessful** — the number of visits that did not result in an order or sale.

**Unplanned visit** — the number of visits that were not planned but were conducted.



**Business benefit**

Using this report, you can:

* monitor the visit activities of sales agents
* assess the work efficiency of agents
* compare planned and completed visits
* analyze sales conducted through visits
* effectively manage the activities of sales agents
