# Services

<details open>

<summary>Services</summary>

Through the Services section, services that are sold or provided in the system are created and managed. This section works similarly to products, but inventory is not tracked for services.

To create a service, the user navigates from the left menu to **Warehouse → Services** and clicks the "Create" button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (390).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The main difference of the Services section is that, unlike products, no balance (stock) is calculated here. That is, services are not received into or issued from the warehouse — they are only sold or provided.

Basic Information\
At this stage, the general information of the service is entered:

* **Name** — the service name is entered
* **Category** — the relevant category for the service is selected
* **Unit of Measure** — an appropriate unit is selected for the service type (for example: hourly, piece, service, and others)
* **SKU** — unique identifier for the service
* **Barcode** — if available, the barcode is entered
* **Note** — additional information (optional)
* **File Upload** — attach a document or file related to the service (optional)

Organizations (pricing settings)\
Here, the service pricing and availability are configured:

* **Availability** — specifies which organization the service is active in
* **Organization** — which branch or company the service will be used in
* **Currency** — the currency in which the price is calculated
* **Store Price** — the main selling price
* **Purchase Price** — cost price (if tracked)
* **Retail** — retail price
* **Wholesale** — wholesale price

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (391).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The importance of services in the system:

* enables managing services separately from products
* does not affect inventory balances
* convenient for service businesses (for example: repair, consulting, service provision)
* supports selling based on time or service units

For example:\
If a "Computer Repair" service is created, it is not linked to the warehouse. The user only sets the price and selects this service during sales.
