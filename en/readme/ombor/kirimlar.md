# Receipts

<details open>

<summary>Receipts</summary>

The Receipts section is used to perform the process of receiving products into the warehouse. This process is typically carried out based on previously created and finalized purchase documents.

When creating a receipt, the user selects a **finalized purchase that does not have direct receipt enabled** from those available in the system. Once a purchase is selected, the list of products related to that purchase is automatically generated and loaded into the receipt window.

Navigate to **Warehouse → Receipts** section and click the **"Create"** button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (42).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### **Basic Information**

At this stage, the general information of the receipt document is entered:

* Receipt number
* Receipt date
* Responsible person
* Supplier
* Warehouse
* Currency
* Notes and file (optional)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (43).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### **Products**

Here the **Purchase** is selected and the products related to the selected purchase appear automatically.

The user can:

* verify the quantity
* modify if necessary
* perform a partial receipt

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (44).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### **Additional Expenses**

Transportation, customs, and other additional expenses are entered here.\
These expenses are added to the product cost.
