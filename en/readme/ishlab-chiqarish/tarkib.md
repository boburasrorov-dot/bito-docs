# Composition

<details open>

<summary>Creating Composition</summary>



The **Composition section** allows you to manage all raw materials, finished products, and additional costs required for manufacturing a product in one place. This enables you to create a complete recipe for the product and automate the production process. This section is used for calculating production costs, controlling resources, and standardizing processes.

Access Production → **Composition** section and add a new composition through the **"Create"** button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (16).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

In this section, all compositions are displayed in table view.

**Table columns:**

* **Number** – composition sequence number
* **Code** – unique code for the composition
* **Name** – composition (recipe) name
* **Description** – additional information
* **Active** – active or inactive status
* **Group** – category to which the composition belongs



When creating a composition, the following information is entered:

* **Code** – optional identifier
* **Name** – composition name (mandatory)
* **Parent group** – assignment to category
* **Description** – additional explanation
* **Status** – active / inactive
* **Organization** – which branches it will be used in

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (22).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The composition contains 3 main sections:

#### **Raw Materials**

Here, the main products used in production are added.

* **Product name** – raw material
* **Unit of measure** – kg, liter, etc.
* **Quantity** – amount consumed
* **Alternatives** – substitute products

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (23).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### **Finished Product**

Products obtained as a result of production are specified.

* **Product name**
* **Unit of measure**
* **Quantity**
* **Percentage** – if enabled, production share is calculated as a percentage

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (24).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### **Additional Costs**

Additional costs related to production are entered.

* Electricity
* Transportation
* Labor costs and others



During system operation:

* Raw materials are automatically reduced from warehouse
* Finished products are added to warehouse
* Additional costs are added to product cost
