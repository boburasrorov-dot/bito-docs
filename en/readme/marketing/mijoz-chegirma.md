# Customer Discount

<details open>

<summary>Creating Customer Discount</summary>

When entering **Marketing → Customer Discount**, a list of all discounts assigned to customers created in the system is displayed.

Through customer discount, special discounts can be granted to specific customers or customer groups. For example, if a regular customer is given a 5% discount, this discount is automatically applied every time they make a purchase. This helps in working individually with customers and retaining them.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (305).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



To create a new customer discount, the Create button is pressed and the following information is entered:

• **Name** — the discount name is entered, which serves to distinguish and identify it in the system.

• **Type** — the discount type is selected (percentage or amount), which determines how the discount is calculated.

• **Amount** — the discount value is entered (for example, 5% or a specific amount), which indicates the discount amount to be given to the customer.

• **Currency** — specifies in which currency the discount is calculated.

• **Duration** — the validity period of the discount is selected (permanent or temporary).

• **Start Date** — if temporary is selected, indicates when the discount starts to be valid.

• **End Date** — specifies until when the discount is valid.

• **Assignment Type** — determines whether the discount applies to all customers or only to selected customers.

• **Auto-assign** — if enabled, the discount is automatically applied during sales.

• **Manual Grant** — allows manual application of discount by the user.

• **Upload File** — additional documents related to the discount are attached.

As a result, the discount is applied automatically or manually during the sales process for selected customers. This helps increase customer loyalty and boost sales through an individual approach.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (312).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

At the bottom is a list of customers, where customers to whom the discount will be applied are selected. Customers can be selected manually or categorized.

After all information is entered, the discount is saved and becomes valid for the selected customers.

As a result, the discount assigned to the customer is applied automatically or manually during the sales process. This serves to improve customer relations and increase sales.
