# Expected Products



<details open>

<summary>Working with Expected Products</summary>

When accessing **Marketing → Expected Products**, a list of all expected products created in the system is displayed.

This section allows you to pre-register products that are expected to arrive at the warehouse in the future. This mainly works based on customer orders.

For example, if a customer requests a specific product and it is not currently available in the warehouse, the product and its quantity are entered through this section and the customer is linked to it. Later, when this product arrives at the warehouse, the customer can be notified through the system.

If the BITO system is integrated with Eskiz.uz or other SMS platforms, an automatic SMS notification is sent to the customer when the product arrives at the warehouse.

To add a new expected product, press the Create button and enter the following information:

• **Product** — select the expected product.

• **Quantity** — enter the quantity expected for this product.

• **Customer** — select the customer waiting for the product.

• **Note** — additional information or reminders can be entered.

• **Upload File** — attach documents related to the product or order.

After all information is entered, it is saved and monitored in the system.

As a result, the ability to notify the customer when the product arrives at the warehouse is created. This helps improve customer relations and prevents missing sales opportunities.
